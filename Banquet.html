About Our Banquet Hall

Our banquet hall in Varanasi is a premier venue for celebrations and gatherings, celebrated for its exquisite design and unique atmosphere. The spacious hall features stunning decor and versatile seating arrangements, making it perfect for weddings, corporate events, and special occasions. The culinary experience is equally remarkable, showcasing a harmonious blend of Asian and European influences. Our talented chefs create a tempting selection of dishes that range from classic favorites to innovative creations, ensuring a memorable dining experience for every guest. For those who appreciate fine dining, our Wine and Cheese Cellar, with its breathtaking five-meter-high glass walls, offers an impressive array of selections. Our knowledgeable staff is on hand to guide you through some of India’s finest culinary treasures, making your event truly unforgettable. Host your next event in a space that reflects the rich cultural heritage of Varanasi and enjoy exceptional service and gourmet cuisine.

Facilities

  • Elegant Atmosphere: Beautifully designed space that sets the perfect tone for any event.

  • Exquisite Cuisine: A diverse menu featuring a blend of Asian and European flavors, crafted by our talented chefs.

  • Versatile Seating: Flexible arrangements to accommodate various event sizes and styles.

  • Wine and Cheese Cellar: An impressive selection of fine wines and cheeses, showcased behind stunning glass walls.

  • Dedicated Service: A knowledgeable staff committed to ensuring a seamless and memorable experience for all guests.
